Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Data Processing

HAR Analyzer is a client-side focused tool. When you "upload" a HAR file, it is processed in your browser memory or temporarily parsed in volatile memory on our servers for the sole purpose of visualization.

We do not store, save, or archive your HAR files. Once the analysis session is closed or the page is refreshed, all data is permanently discarded.

2. Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand basic usage patterns (e.g., number of visitors). This may store a cookie on your device. You can opt-out by disabling cookies in your browser.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@haranalyzer.es.
